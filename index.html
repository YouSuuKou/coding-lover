Introduction

While you are enjoying the convenience of internet, using the tools to create files, or just playing games or listening to the music, do you know how many developers have built and are maintaining the internet and softwares? Today, I'd like to share my interest—developing.

Start

When time goes back to 2023.09.01, I first touched coding named Scratch. Quickly, I fall in love with controling how the code runs. On the first IT class, I learnt C++, found it was much more interesting than Scratch. In 1 year, I learnt crypto, concurrency, fstream, module, and even touched template. To support my developing interest, I studied Calculus, was more hard to learn English and law. The most valuable project I have developed is crypt.

Projects

Why do I said that crypt is the most valuable? On 2026.02.15, the day before New Year's Eve, my classmate in primary school posted a negative message that described how her parents viewed her privacy and said that she wanted to kill herself to keep away from pains. First, I explained how the world beautiful it is, but no effects echoed. She still said that how the death could solve her problems. In anxiety, I found a way to help her. She disgusted her parents check her messages, didn't her? As the result, I developed this project to protect her privacy and published to GitHub.

This crypto software isn't good so far, because it is just an encoder, translate contents into Unicode. It isn't safe at all! However, it actually saved the classmate's life. I plan to update AES-256 crypto algorithm and remake the core by C++ for this project. It recorded how I saved others' life by lending a hand. I will never forget that.

Chasing Star

In the developing journey, I am also chasing a star in my mind—Linus Torvalds. In 1991, he published the biggest open-source project—Linux. Today, almost all servers like BiliBili, YouTube, Tencent use Linux operating system because it is open-source and safe, as well as Linus Torvalds never demand users any money. Although he only gets meager donation, Linus Torvalds still maintains Linux. In your mind, Microsoft and Apple are very modern, right? And this is Linux headquarter. As his saying goes, "I don't have anything against people making money. I just don't want to be the one who has to care about it."

Dream

You know Miku and Luo Tianyi, right? They were generated by Japanese software VOCALOID. So my dream is to learn Machine Learning and algorithm, develop an open-source voice generator that belongs to our motherland, and be a selfless contributor like Linus Torvalds.
